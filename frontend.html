📚 Uttarakhand Exam Subjects UKPSC / UKSSC
📌 UKPSC/UKSSC Exam Pattern: General Studies (History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science), Current Affairs, Uttarakhand State specific History/Culture/Geography, General Hindi. UKPSC PCS has 2 papers; UKSSC Group C/D has General Intelligence + General Awareness.
📚 Uttar Pradesh Exam Subjects UPPSC / UP Police
📌 UPPSC/UP Police Exam Pattern: General Studies (History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Environment), UP Specific GK, General Hindi, Reasoning, Elementary Mathematics. UPPSC PCS has Pre + Mains; UP Police Constable has GK + Hindi + Reasoning + Maths.
📚 SSC Exam Subjects CGL / CHSL / MTS / GD
📌 SSC Exam Pattern: SSC CGL: Tier 1 (GI&R + GK + Quant + English); Tier 2 (Maths + English). SSC CHSL/MTS: GI + GK + Quant + English. SSC GD: GK + GI + Maths + Hindi/English. All exams are computer-based.
